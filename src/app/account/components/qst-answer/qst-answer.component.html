Frequently Asked Questions

To open a new bank account, you can visit any of our branch locations and speak with a customer service representative. They will guide you through the account opening process and help you choose the right account type for your needs. Please ensure you bring the required identification documents and proof of address.

To transfer funds between your accounts, you can log in to our online banking portal or mobile app. From there, navigate to the "Transfers" section and follow the instructions to initiate the transfer. You'll need to provide the source and destination account details along with the transfer amount. Alternatively, you can also visit any of our branch locations and request assistance from a customer service representative.

If your card is lost or stolen, it's important to act quickly to protect your account. Please contact our customer support hotline immediately to report the incident. Our team will assist you in blocking the lost or stolen card and guide you through the process of ordering a replacement card. It's crucial to keep your card details safe and never share them with anyone.
